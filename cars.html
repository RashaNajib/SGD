Cars & Air Pollution

Vehicle exhaust and road traffic are major sources of urban air pollution.

Back to Home

Pollutants from cars

  • Nitrogen oxides (NOx)
  • Carbon monoxide (CO)
  • Particulate matter (especially from diesel)
  • VOCs (which can help form ozone/smog)

Why cities feel it more

Congestion, frequent braking/acceleration, and areas where tall buildings trap air can increase pollution near where people live and walk.

How to reduce it

  • Public transport, carpooling, walking, and cycling
  • Electric vehicles and cleaner fuel standards
  • Regular vehicle maintenance and emission checks
  • Traffic management to reduce congestion
