Fossil Fuels & Air Pollution

Burning coal, oil, and gas for electricity, heating, and industry releases pollutants into the air.

Back to Home

Where it comes from

  • Power plants
  • Heating and cooking (especially with poor ventilation)
  • Industrial boilers and generators

What it releases

  • SO₂ and NOx (can lead to smog and acid rain)
  • PM2.5 (fine particles harmful to lungs and heart)
  • CO₂ (drives climate change, which can worsen air quality events)

How to reduce it

  • Renewable energy (solar, wind) and storage
  • Energy efficiency (buildings, appliances)
  • Cleaner industrial systems and emission controls
  • Transition plans that protect communities and workers
