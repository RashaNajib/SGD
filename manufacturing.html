Manufacturing & Air Pollution

Factories and industrial processes can release gases and particles that reduce air quality and harm health.

Back to Home

Common pollutants

  • Particulate matter (PM2.5 / PM10)
  • Nitrogen oxides (NOx) and sulfur dioxide (SO₂)
  • Volatile organic compounds (VOCs)

Why it happens

High-temperature processes, fuel combustion, chemical reactions, and dust from material handling can all contribute to emissions—especially when controls are weak or outdated.

How to reduce it

  • Filters/scrubbers and better capture systems
  • Cleaner fuels and electrification where possible
  • Efficiency upgrades and regular maintenance
  • Stronger monitoring and enforcement
